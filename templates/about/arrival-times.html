Arrival & Departure Times

  • The site is open to the public from: Thursday 2nd June 10am
  • It closes at: Monday 7th June 12pm (midday)

More detailed times

Thursday 2nd June 10am -- Gates open. You can be on site from now; it's intended for folks to have a chance to set up their tents, find one another and get comfortable. (PS, in the UK this is a bank holiday).

Friday 3rd June 12pm (midday) -- Talks start on our stages (PS, in the UK this is also bank holiday).

Sunday 5th June (evening) -- Last talks on our stages, exact time TBC.

Sunday 5th June (late) -- Music finishes on our stages, exact time TBC.

Monday 7th June 12:00 (mid-day) -- Sites closes to the public.

Set up & tear down

The site is not open before Thursday @ 10am. A number of people will be on-site before then setting things up (e.g. volunteers, villages and installations). To be on site during the set-up you must have organised it with us,and received confirmation, before you arrive. Please, do not turn up before Thursday unless we're expecting you.

Similarly, from Midday Monday 7th we will be tearing down the site and you will need to have arranged this with us.
